https://usa.kaspersky.com/resource-center/definitions/what-is-cloud-security/ Kaspersky Lab The field of cybersecurity known as "cloud security" is concerned with protecting cloud computing systems and guaranteeing the confidentiality and integrity of data on online platforms, apps, and infrastructure. It entails cooperative efforts between cloud service providers and their clientele, which might include both individuals and large corporations. With the goal of safeguarding cloud computing environments, apps, and stored data, this all-encompassing discipline combines best practices, protocols, and technology. While cloud service providers largely deal with backend development to reduce security flaws, customers are essential in helping to ensure that services are configured securely and encouraging responsible usage. In order to improve overall security posture, customers should also make sure that end-user devices and networks are properly secured.
